Last updated: October 2025 • supabase + firebase notifications
Secure chat built with Supabase for data & Firebase Cloud Messaging for notifications

Information We Collect

ChiX collects the minimal information required to provide chat functionality. This includes:

  • Account Information: email, username, profile picture (if you provide one).
  • Chat Data: messages, images, and files you send or receive — stored in Supabase.
  • Device Token: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token used to deliver push notifications to your device.

We do not collect phone contacts, precise geolocation, or other sensitive personal data without explicit consent.

How We Use Your Data

We use the data to:

  • Allow you to sign in and manage your ChiX account.
  • Deliver real-time chat messages and push notifications.
  • Store media and message history securely in Supabase.

ChiX does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

ChiX uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — for authentication, database, and file storage.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) — for push notifications.

These services have their own privacy policies and security measures. We encourage you to review them if you want details about how they process data.

Data Security

We implement standard security practices to protect your data. Your messages and media are stored on Supabase servers and access is restricted to authorized systems only.

Children’s Privacy

ChiX is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. If we make material changes, the "Last updated" date will be revised. Continued use of ChiX after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
